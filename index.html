About Us

With over 25 years experience we are a small family catering company that offers Bespoke Menus ranging from Plated Food, Buffets, BBQ, Street Food. We have catered for events across Cheshire, Merseyside, Lancashire and North Wales.

Our aim is to deliver an individual product to each booking whatever the size. Anything from a celebratory dinner at home to corporate/business events, street food & even weddings are catered for, as we strive to tailor your menu as uniquely as possible. Every booking is a journey to achieving an individually created event.
